Falafel Shawarma

This falafel shawarma recipe brings together crispy, spiced falafel balls wrapped in warm pita bread with fresh vegetables and a creamy tahini sauce. A delicious and satisfying meal!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried chickpeas, soaked overnight
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 4 pita breads
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1/4 cup tahini sauce
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Method

  1. Rinse and drain the chickpeas that have been soaked. Put them in a food processor together with the coriander, cumin, parsley, onion, and garlic.
  2. After pulse, mixture should be ground but not pureed. Small balls or patties that will form.
  3. Heat 2 inches of vegetable oil in deep skillet on medium. Plunge in batches of falafel balls for 2-3 minutes until golden brown. Drain on paper towels.
  4. Place pita breads in a low oven (300⁰F / 150⁰C) and warm for 5 minutes.
  5. In the center of each pita place a few falafel balls. Shred lettuce, tomato slices and drizzle with a teaspoon of tahini sauce.
  6. In the center of each pita place a few falafel balls. Shred lettuce, tomato slices and drizzle with a teaspoon of tahini sauce.
  7. Extra tahini sauce can be placed on the side and enjoyed immediately!
